Seasons Greetings!

This winter, let us be sure to spend plenty of time with the ones you love and cherish!

With an abundance of fun and festive things to do with your loved ones, the holidays are always a blast, but some traditions may be looking to arise.

Here are a few ideas of things to do this holiday season!

  • 1. Visit the Monument in downtown Indianapolis: Enjoy a cup of Peppermint White Hot Chocolate (see menu for recipe) while admiring the christmas decorations!
  • 2. Travel to Perfect North Slopes: This is the best place for tubing, skiing and snowboarding all winter long!
  • 3. Throw a Christmas party: Invite family and friends for fun games, food and drinks! Need ideas? Check out the menu!

Happy Holidays!
