Gingerbread Latte

Ingredients:

  • Espresso, Gingerbread syrup, milk, nutmeg, cinnamon, whipped cream

Steps:

  • Steam your milk of choice
  • Add gingerbread syrup into a mug
  • Pour the milk into the mug and stir
  • Top it off with whipped cream, cinnamon and nutmeg and enjoy!

Peppermint White Hot

Ingredients:

  • Any milk of your choice, white chocolate syrup/sauce, peppermint syrup, whipped cream

Steps:

  • Microwave, boil or steam the milk
  • Mix the peppermint and white chocolate syrup in a mug
  • Carefully pour the milk into the mug and mix
  • Add whipped cream and enjoy!

Hot Apple Cider

Ingredients:

  • Steamed apple cider, cinnamon, maple syrup, peeled oranges and lemons, whole cloves and allspice berries

Steps:

  • 1. Mix the apple cider and maple syrup into a steel pan
  • 2. Then, add in the cloves, peels and berries into the pan
  • 3. Put the pan over medium heat for 5-10 minutes
  • 4. Serve and enjoy!

Eggnog

Ingredients:

  • Milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, egg yolks, vanilla extract, heavy cream, sugar, bourbon or rum

Steps:

  • 1. In a small saucepan, bring the milk, cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla to a boil
  • 2. Whisk egg yolks and sugar in another small pan and then slowly add in the boiled mixture
  • 3. Return mixture to a boil
  • 4. Remove from heat, stir in heavy cream and bourbon/rum
  • 5. Chill in the refrigerator and then serve!

Peppermint Frappe

Ingredients:

  • Vanilla ice cream, chocolate peppermint patties, milk, ice

Steps:

  • 1. Blend peppermint patties, milk, ice and the ice cream in a blender until smooth
  • 2. Pour into a glass and top with crushed peppermint patties, chocolate drizzle and whipped cream
  • 3. Serve and enjoy!
